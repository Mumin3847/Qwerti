FAQ
Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about earning roles in Qwerti.

💬 Discord Roles
How do Discord level roles get assigned?+
Roles like Explorer, Active, Grinder, and Degen are assigned automatically by the MEE6 bot the moment you hit the level milestone. No application needed — just keep chatting!
What's the difference between Legend and Qwertian?+
Legend is manually reviewed and given to members with consistent, high-quality engagement. Qwertian requires you to already hold @Legend and go further — supporting members, sharing educational content across platforms, and staying active on the app.
Can I hold multiple roles at once?+
Yes! Roles stack across different categories. You can hold a Discord level role, a trading volume role, and an activity role simultaneously. The more you do, the more roles you accumulate.
🔁 DEX Roles
How is my trading volume tracked?+
Your trading volume is tracked automatically on Qwerti DEX once your wallet is connected. Open the app and check your profile dashboard to see your current stats and progress toward the next tier.
Is volume tracked cumulatively or does it reset?+
Volume is cumulative — it adds up over time and never resets. Every swap you make permanently contributes to your total, so even small trades count toward your progress.
Do swap count and volume roles work independently?+
Yes, they are separate role tracks. You can earn a swap count role (like Rookie Trader) and a volume role (like Shrimp) at the same time, even if the swap count doesn't match the volume tier directly.
🤝 Referrals
What counts as a successful referral?+
A referral counts when someone signs up on Qwerti DEX using your unique referral link and completes at least one trade on the platform. Simply signing up without trading may not count.
Where's the best place to share my referral link?+
Anywhere! X (Twitter), Telegram, Discord, Reddit, YouTube, personal blogs — the more places you share, the more referrals you'll accumulate. Be genuine about why you enjoy Qwerti.
⚡ Special Roles
What exactly is the Cryptan mystery role?+
The criteria are intentionally secret. Selection is unpredictable and only the most genuine community members have a chance. Stay active on Discord, trade on the DEX, refer friends, and create content. Maybe you'll get it.
How do I become an Ambassador?+
The Ambassador role is assigned directly by the Qwerti core team. Having a strong, active X profile and being a visible, trusted member of the community is key. You can't apply — you get noticed.
What is the Copilot Challenge for the Oracle role?+
A community event where members compete to submit the best trading signal using Qwerti's Magic Links. The winner earns the Oracle role. Watch the Discord announcements for when the next challenge goes live.
How can I earn the Protocol Builder role?+
Share a genuine product idea with the Qwerti team through Discord or direct outreach. If the dev team decides to implement your idea into the platform, you earn the Protocol Builder role.
